TMDA Configuration Overview

  1. First read PreConfiguration for changes you might need to make to non-TMDA software such as your SMTP-server.

  2. Next read ServerConfiguration for how to filter incoming messages on your mailserver with TMDA.

  3. If you want to customize your TMDA auto responses, read the TemplateHowto.

  4. If you want to tag outgoing messages from your mail or news client, then read ClientConfiguration.

  5. If you want to customize your TMDA installation, read ConfigurationVariables. This is also highly recommended reading if you want to get a better sense of TMDA's capabilities and features.

  6. Reading the TmdaFaq is also recommended for a better understanding of TMDA.

  7. If you are upgrading from an earlier release of TMDA, read TmdaUpgrade.

  8. Finally, consider reading one or more of the MailingLists to stay current with TMDA.

  9. If you decide to use TMDA in a product or service offering, consider adding your name to the list.

  10. If you find TMDA useful, consider making a donation.
