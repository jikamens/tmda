Tables

Tables appear if you separate the content of the columns by  ||. All fields of the same row must be also on the same line in the editor.

Markup: 

  • ||<rowbgcolor="#FFFFE0"> '''Column 1'''  |||| '''Column 2 & 3''' ||
||<)> 1  ||<:> 2 ||<(> 3 ||

Display:

  • Column 1

    Column 2 & 3

    1

    2

    3

For details see HelpOnTables.
