TMDA Donations

Like most open source projects, TMDA is developed and supported entirely by the unpaid and free hours of hackers and users from all over the globe. It's entirely a volunteer effort. TMDA is completely free, and you are not obligated to offer anything at all in return for it, but often users prefer to give back in some manner.

Typically, TMDA donations come in the form of valuable non-financial contributions such as code, documentation, and advocacy, but the option of a monetary donation is now available as well. Your financial contribution will be used to further or support the project.

If you feel TMDA has been useful to yourself or your organization and would like to make a donation, click on the button below to pay by credit card.

If you'd prefer to not get involved with Paypal and would like to donate via check, money order, or wire transfer instead, please contact me with the details.
