TMDA Logos

The official TMDA logo was created and donated by Summersault website development after being chosen from among 40 different submissions in the TMDA logo contest. You can read more about the logo in Summersault's press release.

The logo is featured in items at the TMDA store.

Web Logos

Logos of various sizes are provided here for linking and web use.

Large Logos

Larger and higher quality versions of the logo are provided here for artists who might wish to use the logo to create TMDA buttons and other images. If you do decide to do this, feel free to link to your images and/or display them here.
